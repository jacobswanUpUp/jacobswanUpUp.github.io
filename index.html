Through the computational mechanism underlying human brain towards computer perception

Leave a reply

An article about what I’m thinking recently , please access it here : PDF

Abstract :

“This article tries to trace some important principles lay behind deep learning back to computational neuroscience. I will first review some theoretical results for neural encoding, followed by the discussion about auto-encoder architecture built upon those results, to show sparse coding is a strategy that is plausible both for nervous system and for feature learning. Then I will discuss the same computational mechanism employed by both human visual cortex and deep convolutional network, namely learning representation with multiple, hierarchical layers. This article aims to highlight the unification of computational modeling of human brain and building machines that could act as intelligently as human do.”
